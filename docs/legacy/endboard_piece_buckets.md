# Endboard Piece Buckets (Goldfish)

## Definition
An endboard piece is a target outcome for goldfish combo search. It includes both:
- On-field cards that represent a payoff or lock.
- Required graveyard conditions that represent a live resource state (e.g., trap pieces in GY).

Scope: turn-player only, no opponent interruption or interaction.

## Buckets
- S: primary best endboard target
- A: near-best primary payoff
- B: strong secondary payoff / support endboard

## Seeded Bucket Contents

### S
- D/D/D Wave High King Caesar (alias: "Caesar")

### A
- Fiendsmith's Desirae (note: needs equip spell / links equipped to be online)

### B
- A Bao A Qu, the Lightless Shadow
- Fiendsmith's Rextremende
- S:P Little Knight
- GY_CONDITION: either Fiendsmith Kyrie in GY OR Fiendsmith in Paradise in GY

Note:
